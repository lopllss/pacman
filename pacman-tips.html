Pacman Hacks and Tricks!

Here are a few tips and strategies to make you a better player



Pacman might feel like a simple game, but it’s actually very intense and challenging. It’s the type of title that will always catch you off-guard just because every board is different. But with the right Pacman strategy and with a healthy dose of experimentation you can get amazing results. In the end it’s all about being committed and having fun.

Track the ghosts

Since they are coming to get you, it’s important to stay away from them. It works in everything from the Pacman original to the Pacman online and many others. The trick is that you want to track the ghosts as fast as possible and stay away from them. If they come too close to you, then there are some methods to get rid of them. There are only 4 ghosts, so managing them is a lot easier.

Get all the points by going in a continuous line

Shifting all around might work in the Pacman online game, but that will increase the time you need. Which is why the best trick you can have is to go in a straight line as much as possible. Sure, when the ghosts are on you it might be hard to do it. But when you have the chance, do that. The idea is to acquire as many points as you can for the best results.

Use the sides

You can go from the left side to the right if you want. It might not feel that important, but it does work well and the nice thing is that you can always commit to whatever idea and strategy you want. All of that can be really rewarding, as long as you know how to manage and handle everything in a meaningful way.

Try various patterns

The trick is to see a pattern that works and use it based on the level. Some people used the cherry pattern in the Pacman original and it worked well for them. Others go with the apple pattern, as that’s very helpful too. Don’t hesitate to experiment with patterns and even create your own if you want.

Use trick zones

These are at the upper right or bottom right as well as the tunnels. You can use these with the idea of misguiding the ghosts. Of course, as soon as you used the ghosts you want to get to the desired location as fast as possible. Because if you’re not able to do that, you will most likely lose.

The idea with Pacman online is that you always need to evade and come up with a great Pacman strategy. Every time you play you can try out something new and interesting, it all comes down to not having a stale approach. It’s a great opportunity for players and it will work pretty well. Yes, it’s not the simplest option, but as long as you strive to come up with new ideas it might help quite a bit. Yet you have to realize that Pacman is a game about experimenting and trying new things. So that should be a great motivator as you play!
